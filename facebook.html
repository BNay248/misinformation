Facebook is the most commonly used social media app, with almost 170 million users in the United States. With so many users posting so often, it's impossible ot monitor the entire site on your own. Facebook states that they have set the following goals for it's handling of misinformation (facebook.com):

  • disrupting economic incentives because most false news is financially motivated
  • building new products to curb the spread of false news
  • helping people make more informed decisions when they encounter false news

In 2016 Denzel Washington was said to have spoken out against Barack Obama in a fake article. Several sources including Denzel Washinton himself have since debunked the story, but not before the story spread like wildfire on Facebook, acting of one of the first motivators for Facebook to pay more attention to reported posts.
