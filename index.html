Over the past two years there has been innumerable amounts of misinformation in social media and news outlets. Misinformation is intentionally presented in a way that is difficult to distinguish from reliable information. This website aims to help you identify what information is reliable, what tricks people use to relay misinformation, and debunk common misconceptions about censorship and misinformation online.
So what is misinformation?

Information has never been more fluid or easily accessible than it is today. The tranfer of information over the web is one of the most important pieces of technology humans have ever used. With this easy flow of information, it is important to discuss how we deal with information that we know is wrong, and how we deal with information that we disagree with. You can find examples from Twitter, News sources, and Facebook on this website.
